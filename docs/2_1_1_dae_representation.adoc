==== DAE representation

There are different forms od DAEs

[implicit-dae]
===== Implicit DAE

[latexmath]
++++
F(\dot{x}, x, t) = 0
++++


[semi-explicit-dae]
===== Semi-Explicit DAE

[latexmath]
++++
\begin{split}
\dot{x} & = f(x, s, t)    \\
0       & = g^1 (x, s, t) \\
0       & = g^2 (x, s, t) \\
0       & = g^3 (x, s, t) \\
        & \vdots
\end{split}
++++
